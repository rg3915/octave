# octave

Octave exercises

    octave:1> A = [1,2,3; 4,5,6; 7,8,9]
    A =

       1   2   3
       4   5   6
       7   8   9

    octave:2> A'
    ans =

       1   4   7
       2   5   8
       3   6   9

    octave:3> B = [2 3 4; 5 6 7; 8 9 10]
    B =

        2    3    4
        5    6    7
        8    9   10

    octave:4> A + B
    ans =

        3    5    7
        9   11   13
       15   17   19
